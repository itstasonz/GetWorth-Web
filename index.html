The Problem

A $200B Market Running
on Guesswork

The second-hand economy is massive — and completely broken. Sellers don't know what their items are worth. Buyers can't trust what they see. And everyone wastes time on platforms built for a different era.

Unknown Item Value

People have no idea what their belongings are actually worth in today's market. Items sit unused or get sold for a fraction of their value.

Scams & Unsafe Trading

Fake listings, misleading photos, and dishonest sellers plague every marketplace. Buyers have no reliable trust signals before transacting.

Price Confusion

No standard exists for second-hand pricing. The same item can be listed at wildly different prices, creating distrust and friction in every transaction.

Fragmented Market

No unified platform exists that combines product identification, fair valuation, and secure trading in a single seamless experience.

The Solution

AI That Knows What
Things Are Worth

GetWorth combines computer vision, market intelligence, and a trust layer to create the standard infrastructure for the second-hand economy.

AI Product Identification

Snap a photo or upload an image. Our AI identifies the exact brand, model, and specs — including OCR for serial and model numbers.

Real-Time Market Pricing

Every valuation is powered by aggregated market data. You'll see low, mid, and high price points with confidence scoring.

Verified & Secure Trading

Seller verification, quality scoring, buyer confirmation, and order tracking create a trust layer that eliminates scams.

Personal Asset Portfolio

Track the value of everything you own in one place. Watch items appreciate or depreciate over time. Know your net worth.
